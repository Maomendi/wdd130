Colombia

Colombia is a country located in the northwest corner of South America, bordered by the Caribbean Sea to the north and the Pacific Ocean to the west. It possesses diverse landscapes, including the Andes mountains, rainforests, and awesome coastlines. Colombia is known for its rich cultural heritage, influenced by Indigenous, African, and Spanish traditions. Bigger cities include Bogotá, Medellín, Cali, and Cartagena, each offering unique experiences in terms of history, architecture, and cuisine. Colombia is famous thanks to its vibrant music and dance traditions, such as cumbia and salsa.
